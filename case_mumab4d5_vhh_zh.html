Executive Summary

From Full Antibody to Single-Domain VHH Nanobody

A 3-minute overview of the problem, approach, and outcome.

The Challenge

muMAb4D5 is a conventional VH/VL antibody (~150 kDa). For applications requiring tissue penetration, bispecific formatting, or simplified manufacturing, a single-domain VHH nanobody (~15 kDa) is preferred — but directly trimming VL leaves the VH hydrophobic interface exposed, causing aggregation.

Our Approach

Therasik's VHH conversion pipeline: two-pathway head-to-head design (IGHV1-69 vs IGHV3-23 frameworks), systematic hallmark residue grafting, SASA surface reshaping to eliminate the exposed VL interface, ABodyBuilder2 structure prediction, HADDOCK3 docking validation, and full CMC benchmarking against 39 clinical VHH sequences.

Key Outcome

Structural RMSD of 0.054 Å vs parent. All 3 CDRs preserved with 100% identity. DockQ score 0.671 confirming HER2 binding. Outperforms the only clinical anti-HER2 VHH on key CMC metrics. A rationally designed single-domain antibody ready for downstream formatting.

Background

Why Convert VH to VHH Nanobody?

muMAb4D5 — the precursor to Herceptin® — carries three CDRs with nanomolar HER2 affinity. Converting its VH domain into a humanized VHH nanobody unlocks a smaller, more versatile molecular format while preserving its binding specificity.

VHH Advantages over IgG

  • ~15 kDa vs ~150 kDa — 10× smaller, better tissue penetration
  • Cost-effective production in E. coli or yeast
  • Superior thermal stability (Tm typically >60°C)
  • Modular — ideal for CAR-T, bispecifics, ADC, imaging agents
  • Binds epitopes inaccessible to conventional IgG

muMAb4D5 VH — A Natural VHH Candidate

  • Already carries all 3 Stealth residues (H35, R50, R94) — no engineering needed
  • Near-VHH FR2 hallmark (VGLW) — only L45R required
  • Long CDR2 (17 aa) matches clinically validated HER2 VHH architecture
  • PDB 1FVC verified sequence — no ambiguity in starting material
  • CDRs 100% identical to trastuzumab — proven HER2 binding epitope
Design Strategy

Two Pathways, One Winner

We evaluated two independent humanization strategies head-to-head: IGHV1-69 progressive surface reshaping vs. IGHV3-23 CDR grafting. The result was decisive — higher FR humanization does not always mean better developability.

How to Read the Docking Data
HADDOCK Score −34.6
Weighted sum of van der Waals, electrostatic, and desolvation energy. More negative = stronger interaction. −34.6 indicates a stable, well-packed interface.
DockQ 0.671
Composite quality score (0–1). >0.6 = medium quality, >0.8 = high quality. Combines i-RMSD, L-RMSD, and fnat. 0.671 confirms the pose is physically meaningful.
fnat 76%
Fraction of restraint contacts satisfied. Our AIR restraints were defined from the trastuzumab epitope (1N8Z). 76% recovery means the docked pose respects known biology.
BSA 1524 Å²
Buried Surface Area measures how much surface is hidden at the interface. 1500–2000 Å² is typical for antibody–antigen complexes, confirming a genuine binding interface.
