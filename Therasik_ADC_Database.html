步骤 2 — 半乳糖苷酶After ARSA desulfation, β-galactosidase cleaves the galactose-payload glycosidic bond, releasing the drug. Only the desulfated (ARSA-processed) product is substrate for β-galactosidase → sequential "lock-and-key" mechanism.
设计优势1) Highly hydrophilic (anionic sulfate + pyranose) → superior PK at high DAR. 2) Lysosome-restricted dual-enzymatic requirement → no extracellular premature release. 3) Distinct from cathepsin B-based linkers → no cross-resistance with vc-PABC-based ADCs.
Payload 兼容性Demonstrated with MMAE conjugated via maleimide to trastuzumab. Anti-HER2 ADC showed IC50 ~0.5 nM in HER2+ cells; selective activity (HER2+ vs HER2− cell lines).
血浆稳定性Excellent — neither ARSA nor β-galactosidase are present in plasma; requires sequential lysosomal environment for both steps.
文献来源
原始论文Duret D, Bhatt DK, et al. "A dual-enzyme cleavable linker for antibody–drug conjugates." Chem Commun (RSC) 2021;57:5599. DOI: 10.1039/d1cc00957e. Academic research group; ADC conjugated to trastuzumab (anti-HER2).
专利状态Academic publication (2021); no granted commercial patent identified as of 2026. Proof-of-concept stage. The dual-enzyme cascade concept is not yet protected by a major company; represents an open innovation opportunity for ADC development.
注意This is an early-stage academic ADC linker concept. No clinical ADC using this exact dual-enzyme mechanism has entered Phase I as of April 2026. Included as representative of emerging dual-stimulus linker strategies.

Binding Affinity

目的Measure KD to recombinant antigen and cell-surface antigen. Too high affinity may cause &#x27;binding site barrier&#x27; effect.
方法SPR (Biacore), BLI (Octet), Flow Cytometry (FACS)
方案与灵敏度
