Vaccine Design Knowledge Base

A centralized evidence base for vaccine engineering. Covering tumor antigens, infectious pathogens, and tolerogenic targets with clinical-grade TCR data, delivery platform insights, and mRNA construct rules.

All data is sourced from peer-reviewed literature, clinical trial reports, and verified databases (PDB, IEDB, UniProt). Evidence tiers and verification status are provided for each record.

Tumor Antigen Targets

Curated TAA and TSA database covering expression patterns, MHC-I/II epitopes, HLA restriction, and clinical status to support target prioritization and multi-epitope design.

Infectious Disease Antigens

Key protective antigens from major viral and bacterial pathogens, including approved vaccine references, immune correlates, and variant update strategies.

Autoimmunity / Tolerogenic Targets

Self-antigens involved in autoimmune diseases, including HLA risk associations, clinical status of tolerogenic vaccines, and immune tolerance induction strategies.

TCR-pMHC Database

Clinically relevant TCR clones with full α/β chain sequences, CDR3 motifs, and structural data covering cancer, viral, and neoantigen scenarios.

Verified TCR Clones

Records traceable to PDB structures or peer-reviewed literature (PMID), including affinity data and clinical context. Click card to expand sequence details.

TCR Selection Rules

Principles for selecting TCR clones for immune monitoring, adoptive cell therapy, and cross-reactivity risk assessment.

Public TCR Motifs

High-frequency TCR clonotypes observed across multiple donors, useful for population-level immune response prediction.

Delivery Platforms & Adjuvants

Insights into mRNA, viral vectors, and subunit platforms, including manufacturing notes, cold chain requirements, and immune bias profiles.

Delivery Platforms

Comparison of major vaccine delivery technologies based on clinical validation, process complexity, and logistical requirements.

Adjuvant Systems

Mechanism of action, immune bias (Th1/Th2/Th17), and approved product references for common vaccine adjuvants.

Universal CD4 Helper Epitopes

Broadly reactive CD4⁺ T cell epitopes (e.g., PADRE) used to enhance immune help and overall vaccine immunogenicity.

Design Methodologies

Consolidated rules for multi-epitope construct design, mRNA parameters, and experimental validation gates to support systematic design decisions.

Design Playbooks

Standardized workflows for tumor, infectious, and TCR-guided vaccine projects with clear decision nodes and exit criteria.

mRNA Construct Rules

Guidelines for epitope linkers (AAY, GPGPG), signal peptides, MITD fusion, and junctional neoepitope quality control.

Experimental Gates

Core experimental readouts (ELISPOT, Multimer, ICS, etc.) and stage-gate criteria for immunogenicity validation.

Project Feedback

Internal repository of design decisions and experimental outcomes to continuously refine design rules and model parameters.

Tool Benchmarks

Public benchmark data for MHC-I/II prediction tools (AUC, sensitivity, specificity) to help set rational thresholds for validation.

This knowledge base is powered by peer-reviewed evidence and verified data sources. For custom vaccine design services, please contact the InSynBio / Therasik team.
